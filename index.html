LTP

Literacy Through Photography

LTP is a student-centered teaching methodology that promotes participation, collaboration, creativity, and self-expression through photos and writing. LTP gives students the freedom to share their stories and grow as collaborators, creators, and problem-solvers. Scroll down to learn about the benefits of LTP and check out some example projects.
